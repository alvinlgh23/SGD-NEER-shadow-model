# SGD-NEER-shadow-model
Shadow S$NEER model to approximate Monetary Authority of Singapore exchange rate policy and identify deviations between market FX pricing and policy-implied levels.

## What this model does

This project reconstructs a shadow S$NEER index to approximate the exchange rate policy of the Monetary Authority of Singapore.

By comparing the model-implied level with SGD/USD, it identifies deviations between market pricing and policy-driven exchange rate dynamics.
